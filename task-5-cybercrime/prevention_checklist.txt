Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

1.  Always verify the sender’s email address before clicking on any link
    or downloading attachments.
2.  Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone, even
    if they claim to be from a trusted organization.
3.  Check website URLs carefully – look for “https://” and avoid
    suspicious or misspelled links.
4.  Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your email, social media,
    and banking apps.
5.  Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like
    banking or email.
6.  Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and
    antivirus software.
7.  Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages asking for immediate
    action.

Financial/UPI Safety Tips: 8. Never approve unknown UPI collect requests
or scan QR codes sent by strangers. 9. Double-check the receiver’s name
before sending money via UPI apps. 10. Do not install unknown apps or
screen-sharing apps suggested by unknown callers.

Reporting Cybercrime: • National Cyber Crime Portal:
https://cybercrime.gov.in • Helpline Number: 1930

Stay alert, think before you click, and protect your digital identity!
