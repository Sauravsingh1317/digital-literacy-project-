Task 3: Platforms and Tools

Objective
The objective of this task is to explore and gain hands-on experience with different digital platforms used for learning, coding, and collaboration.

Description
In this task, I worked with multiple digital platforms to perform practical activities. I used Google Forms to design a quiz on digital literacy and collected responses, which were further analyzed using Google Sheets. I also practiced basic coding by solving a beginner-level problem on HackerRank.

Outcome
This task enhanced my understanding of how digital tools are used in real-life scenarios. It also improved my ability to work with online platforms for coding, data collection, and collaboration, which are essential skills for academic and professional growth.
